## What is Mectris?

Running a Minecraft server means flying half-blind. You feel it when the server lags, you hear about it when players leave — but by then it's already too late.

**Mectris turns your server into something you can actually see.** It's a hosted analytics platform that watches your network around the clock and gives you one clean dashboard for everything that matters: how your server is performing, who's playing, and when something goes wrong.

No spreadsheets. No guesswork. No digging through console logs at 2 AM. Just the full picture, live.

---

## Why server owners use it

🎯 **Know your community, not just your player count.**
A number on a server list tells you nothing. Mectris shows you who's new, who keeps coming back, and who quietly drifted away — so you can actually understand the people behind the playerbase.

⚡ **Catch problems before your players do.**
Lag spikes, crashes, and downtime get tracked automatically — with a snapshot of exactly what the server looked like in the moments leading up to each incident. You stop guessing what happened and start knowing.

📈 **Make decisions with real data.**
When are your peak hours? Is the server actually growing? Did that update help retention or hurt it? Mectris answers the questions every owner has but rarely has the numbers for.

🌙 **Peace of mind, even when you're away.**
Get pinged on Discord the moment your server goes down, recovers, or crosses a performance threshold you care about. Mectris keeps watch so you don't have to.

## How it works

Getting started takes minutes, not hours.

1. **Create your server** in the Mectris dashboard.
2. **Connect the plugin** with a single claim token — drop it in, and you're linked.
3. **Watch the data roll in.** Performance, players, and trends start populating right away.

That's it. No databases to manage, no infrastructure to babysit. Mectris handles the heavy lifting so you can focus on your community.
